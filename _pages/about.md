I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Agricultural, Food, and Resource Economics at Michigan State University, with a dual major in Economics. My research focuses on heterogeneity in international trade, the behavior of multinational firms, and how global supply chains are organized. More broadly, I am interested in economic development, including how trade policy affects resource use in agriculture.

I earned my M.A. and B.S. from Seoul National University. Outside of research, I enjoy traveling and riding bicycles.

Contact: [ohsaera@msu.edu](mailto:ohsaera@msu.edu)
