I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Agricultural, Food, and Resource Economics at Michigan State University, with a dual major in Economics. My research focuses on international trade, multinational firms, and global supply chains, and I am broadly interested in economic development and the role of trade in agricultural resource use.

I earned my M.A. and B.S. from Seoul National University. Outside of research, I enjoy traveling and riding bicycles.

Contact: [ohsaera@msu.edu](mailto:ohsaera@msu.edu)
